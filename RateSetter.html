Bid formula: Fuel + Maintenance + (billing days × profit/day) + differentials = floor bid. Recommended bid adds 15%.

Home base is not included in mileage costs. It's used only to flag trips where you can drive home from the drop and arrive by 8 PM — those bids are reduced 15% to help you win them.

Sleep: 1 hr rest per 3 hrs driving, on trips ≥ 15 hrs of drive time.

Billing days are fuzzy: pickup after noon = ½ day; delivery before noon = ½ day; all other days count as full days.

Base trip cost sets a minimum floor — useful for rejecting very short trips that aren't worth the effort.
